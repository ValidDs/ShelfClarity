The problem

Your shelf is not the same as a routine.

Most beauty apps recommend more products. ShelfClarity starts with what you already own, then helps you reduce confusion, overlap, and wasted spend.

1

Too many products

No clear next move

How it works

From shelf photo to routine plan.

The MVP is intentionally simple: upload, confirm, audit, then receive a practical plan.

1

Upload your shelf

Send shelf photos, individual product photos, or label shots. ShelfClarity extracts likely products and categories.

2

Confirm what we found

You verify product names, usage, concerns, budget, and goals so the audit does not pretend uncertainty is fact.

3

Get your audit

Receive keep, pause, replace, use carefully, likely redundant, missing essential, and next-buy guidance.

Trust first

Useful without pretending to be medical.

  • No diagnosis, cure, treatment, or guaranteed result language.
  • Confidence labels show when product data is incomplete.
  • Prescription, pregnancy, severe irritation, and diagnosed conditions route to professional-care guidance.
  • AI explains the report, but structured rules and cautious wording drive the recommendation boundaries.
